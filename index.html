Overview

I am an MD-PhD Candidate in AI & Medicine, bridging the gap between computational intelligence and biological complexity.

With 9+ years of experience in cancer & AI research, I have conducted research at Fudan, CAS, and Stanford. I currently work with Dr. Qiang Gao.

My primary focus lies in AI-driven therapeutic design and deciphering the tumor microenvironment to unlock new possibilities in cancer immunotherapy.
